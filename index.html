ABOUT THE EVENT

3rd Asian Biodynamic Conference & Trainers meeting

The Himalayas are under threat, with their ecological foundations, farmland and glacial lakes - the lifeblood of Asia - disappearing at an alarmimg rate. Could biodynamic agriculture - a holistic, regenerative farming approach - provide cross-cutting solutions to transform our food systems and heal our land?

We invite farmers, biodynamic initiatives ad associations, local multipliers, and key stakeholders from the food sector to join us at the 3rd International Asian Biodynamic Conference, where we will explore synergies and collaborations for the future of agriculture in Asia.

Our Mission

Aiming to discover new perspectives on biodynamic agriculture in Asia, this conference will place special emphasis on holistic approaches and integrated studies.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to reach conference venue?

Kathmandu has the only international airport in Nepal. From there, it takes about 30 minutes to reach Pokhara by domestic flight. Alternatively, travelers often enjoy the scenic tourist bus ride from Kathmandu to Pokhara, which takes around six hours. From the bus park or domestic airport, taxis are readily available to reach Hotel Grande. The fare is below 500 rupees, and the ride takes just¬†a¬†few¬†minutes.

What is included in the conference ticket?

Your ticket covers the full program, 3 nights‚Äô accommodation, 3 breakfasts, 4 lunches, coffee breaks, and 3 dinners.

Can I stay additional nights at the hotel?

Yes, extended stays can be arranged directly with the hotel if you wish to arrive earlier or leave later.

Do I need a visa to enter Nepal?

Most visitors can obtain an on-arrival visa at Tribhuvan International Airport (TIA) or major border entry points.

Are trekking activities included in the ticket?

No, trekking is optional and not included in the ticket. You can reserve it during conference registration.

What language will the conference be conducted in?

The conference will be held in English.
